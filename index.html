How We Operate

Onboarding with IbonAir

IbonAir will verify operator compliance with FAA Part 135.
Following verification, operators will be issued access to an IbonAir Operator Account.

With an IbonAir Operator Account, operators may:
  • Manage fleet
  • Receive and respond to client requests
  • Book flights
  • Showcase fleet in a public profile
  • Post empty legs
  • Receive flight request matches

IbonAir Compliance Policy

IbonAir is a digital marketplace for private air charter services operated by licensed Part 135 air carriers. The platform is owned and managed by TwynTail LLC, a California-registered limited liability company.

Regulatory Scope

IbonAir does not hold itself out as a direct air carrier and does not conduct flight operations. The company operates in accordance with U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) Part 295 guidance for air charter brokering and only partners with operators holding valid FAA Part 135 certificates and DOT economic authority.

Business Formation and Licensing

TwynTail LLC is registered in California and holds all required local business licenses. IbonAir is in the process of completing its California Seller of Travel registration and maintains general liability and errors & omissions insurance coverage.

Operator Verification

Before any operator appears on the platform, IbonAir verifies the operator’s FAA certificate, D085 aircraft listing, DOT Air Taxi registration, and current insurance coverage meeting industry standards.

IbonAir Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect operator and user data (name, contact, certification, documents) for verification and communication. No payment or passenger data is collected until compliant systems are in place.

Data Protection & Security
